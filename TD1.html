Exercice 2

1

On a \( \ln \dfrac{k}{k+1} = \ln k - \ln (k+1) \). C'est une somme téléscopique: \( A_n = 1 - \ln (k+1) \).

2

On a \( \min(k,n) = \left\{\begin{array}{l} k\, \mathtt{ si } \, k\leq n,\\ n\, \mathtt{ si } \, k\geq n\\ \end{array}\right. . \)

On a \( \sum_{k=0}^{2n} \min(k,n) = \sum_{k=0}^n \min(k,n) + \sum_{k=n+1}^{2n} \min(k,n) \),
ce qui donne \(\sum_{k=0}^n k + \sum_{k=n+1}^{2n} n \), ou encore \( \dfrac{n(n+1)}{2} + n*n \).

Finalement, \(B_n = \dfrac{n(3n+1)}{2}\).
